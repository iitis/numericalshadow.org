Shadows of family of matrices
$$
M_2(\phi)=e^{i\phi}
\begin{pmatrix}
0&1\\
0&1
\end{pmatrix},
$$

with $\phi\in [0, 2\pi ]$. Upper and right plots present marginal
distributions of real and imaginary part corresponding to shadows of
hermitian and anti-hermitian parts of $M_2(\phi)$.
