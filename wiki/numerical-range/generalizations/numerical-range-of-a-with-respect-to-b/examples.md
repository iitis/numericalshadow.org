## Examples

### Example 1

Diagonal matrix $A=\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & 0\\\ 0 & 1+2\ii & 0 & 0\\\ 0
& 0 & 3\ii & 0\\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$ with respect to matrix $B
=\1_4$.

assumption, the numerical range $w_{\|\|\cdot\|\|_\infty}(A;\1_4)=
W(A)$.
