Shadows of family of matrices
```math
M_3(\phi)=e^{\ii\phi}
\begin{pmatrix}
-1&0&0\\
1&0&0\\
0&0&\ii
\end{pmatrix},
``` with ``\phi\in [0, 2\pi ]``. Upper and
right plots present marginal distributions of real and imaginary part
corresponding to shadows of hermitian and anti-hermitian parts of
``M_3(\phi)``.
